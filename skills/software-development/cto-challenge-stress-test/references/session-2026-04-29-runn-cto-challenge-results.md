# Runn CTO challenge filter — session notes (2026-04-29)

Source:
- https://www.runn.io/blog/challenges-of-being-a-new-cto

What mattered:
- Ignore the product pitch / CTA sections.
- Extract the underlying CTO challenge, then compare it to the governing CTO manual.

Canonical challenge inventory from the article:
1. Stepping into someone else’s shoes
2. Escaping gravitational operational pull
3. Setting strategic direction
4. Becoming a Babel Fish
5. Prioritizing initiatives and managing expectations
6. Getting stakeholder buy-in
7. Leading change
8. Aligning tech talent to business goals
9. Protecting profitability
10. Shaping culture

Evaluation pattern that worked:
- Strong coverage: 1–5, 8, 10
- Partial coverage: 6, 7, 9
- Main gaps to watch: explicit listening tour artifact, stakeholder map / ownership map, change rollout template, profitability / utilization cadence

Useful red-team takeaway:
- The agentic CTO is resilient on strategy, translation, prioritization, and delegation.
- It can still underperform if it lacks concrete execution artifacts for stakeholder buy-in and change management.
- Profitability needs to be framed with capacity, utilization, and business impact; otherwise it can become too abstract.
